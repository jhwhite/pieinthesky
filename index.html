NOTES ON REHEATING OUR PIZZA

Please do not use a microwave to reheat our pizza! The best way to reheat pizza slices is in a frying pan. It takes longer, but it is worth it. What happens when you nuke a piece of pizza in the microwave is that you excite the food and water molecules which vibrate and generate heat. The water molecules turns into steam and what you get is dry tough pizza. If you put your stove burner on medium-low and put your slice in a frying pan, you heat your pizza gently. Shake it every now and then to keep it from sticking and the crust will get nice and crispy and the cheese and toppings will slowly begin to warm. When the cheese on top is pretty warm, the slice is done. If the bottom gets too brown, the heat is too high. That’s all there is to it, and the pie will taste as good as when you ordered it.
